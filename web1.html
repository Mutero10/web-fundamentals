Web 1.0

Web 1.0 (or "Web 1") is the first iteration of the World Wide Web, spanning roughly the 1990s to early 2000s, characterized as a "read-only" or "static" web. It was primarily informational, featuring non-interactive pages (HTML) where a few content creators provided information for a large audience of passive consumers.
