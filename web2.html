Web 2.0

Web 2.0, or the "participative social web," refers to the second generation of the World Wide Web, characterized by a shift from static, read-only websites to dynamic, interactive platforms. It emphasizes user-generated content, usability, and interoperability, allowing users to actively create, share, and collaborate rather than just consuming information passively.
